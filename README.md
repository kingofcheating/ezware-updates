## Features

<details open>
<summary><strong>Home and navigation</strong></summary>

- Six pages: Home, Aim, Visuals, Movement, Profiles, and Settings
- Connection, safety, active-profile, version, and update status
- Quick links to Visuals and Profiles
- Visual-only and minimal-overlay presets
- Read-only toggle, safe menu hiding, and unload controls

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Aim and trigger tools</strong></summary>

- Configurable aimbot, silent aim, team check, and sticky target
- Head, torso, or legs targeting
- Adjustable FOV size, thickness, color, smoothness, and activation key
- Optional on-screen FOV circle with live preview
- Adjustable silent-aim pulse
- Triggerbot with team check, radius, delay, and cooldown controls
- One-click Aim page reset

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Player overlay</strong></summary>

- ESP master toggle
- Full or corner boxes with thickness, rounding, fill, opacity, and color controls
- Player names with optional anonymization
- Health bars with side, width, and high/low-health colors
- Distance labels with adjustable maximum range and text scale
- Snaplines with origin, thickness, and color controls
- Skeletons with thickness and color controls
- Options for filled boxes and dead players
- Live overlay preview and one-click Visuals reset

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Crosshair and hit feedback</strong></summary>

- FPS display in any screen corner
- Cross, dot, or circle crosshair styles
- Crosshair outline, size, gap, thickness, and color controls
- Hit markers with diagonal, plus, or circle styles
- Marker size, gap, thickness, duration, and color controls
- Ten hit sounds with volume control and instant preview
- One-click feedback reset

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Movement</strong></summary>

- Optional walk-speed override
- Optional jump-power override
- Default, medium, and high presets
- One-click movement reset
- Automatically blocked and restored by read-only mode

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Profiles</strong></summary>

- Create, save, update, load, refresh, and delete profiles
- Automatic startup profile selection
- Active-profile and unsaved-change indicators
- Save-before-exit protection
- Recovery backups and safer bounded file handling
- Automatic legacy-profile migration without deleting originals
- Local storage under `%LocalAppData%\ezware\Vault`

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Appearance, privacy, and diagnostics</strong></summary>

- Read-only mode enabled by default
- Privacy mode hides player and profile names
- Configurable watermark with FPS, profile, and corner options
- Smooth animations with adjustable speed
- Menu scale, opacity, and custom accent color
- Violet, blue, and emerald accent presets
- Configurable notifications with duration, scale, corner, and sound controls
- Connection, process, entity, viewport, frame-time, render-rate, and offset diagnostics
- Animated menu, tabs, controls, tooltips, pulses, and notification stacks

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Performance, updates, and reliability</strong></summary>

- DirectX 11 external overlay without DLL injection
- Display-aware automatic frame pacing from 60 to 240 FPS
- Avoids unnecessary overlay repositioning when the target window is unchanged
- Built-in version checking and release updates
- Rejects incomplete update and offset downloads
- Render-target recovery after DirectX interruptions
- Clean shutdown of process handles, overlay state, and UI backends
- Long-session timer support and accurate embedded version information

</details>

## Quick start

1. Open the [latest release](https://github.com/kingofcheating/ezware-updates/releases/latest).
2. Expand **Assets** and download `ezware.exe`.
3. Launch it on Windows 10 or 11 with a DirectX 11-capable system.
4. Press `Insert` to open the menu.

> [!TIP]
> Start with **Visual-only setup** or leave **Read-only mode** enabled while exploring the interface.

## Controls

| Key | Action |
| :--- | :--- |

| `F10` | Privacy mode |
